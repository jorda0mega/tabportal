# Privacy Policy for Tab Portal

**Last updated:** January 4, 2025

## Overview

Tab Portal is a browser extension that helps you search and switch between open tabs. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**Tab Portal does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

Specifically:
- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is recorded
- No tab data is sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking is implemented
- No cookies are used
- No user accounts are required

## Data Usage

The extension accesses your tab information (titles, URLs, favicons) solely to display the searchable tab list within your browser. This data:
- Is processed entirely locally on your device
- Never leaves your browser
- Is not stored persistently
- Is only accessed when you activate the extension

## Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function:
- **Tabs**: To read tab titles and URLs for the search feature
- **Scripting**: To display the search overlay on the current page
- **Host permissions**: To show the overlay on any webpage you're viewing

These permissions are used exclusively for the extension's core functionality.

## Third Parties

Tab Portal does not share any data with third parties because no data is collected.
